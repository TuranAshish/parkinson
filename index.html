About the program

Small practices that support everyday wellbeing.

This program introduces accessible meditation approaches that may help participants cultivate attention, body awareness, emotional steadiness, and a greater sense of agency.

Every practice is designed to be approachable. Participants can sit, stand, or adapt movements to their comfort and ability.

01
Parkinson's-specific

Examples and pacing are shaped around the lived experience of Parkinson's.

02
Flexible and accessible

Practices can be modified for energy, mobility, and symptom fluctuations.

03
Grounded in science

The professional pathway connects each component to research and publications.
